Montmartre Tours

Guided Tours

Montmartre Art Tour

Take in the artistic heritage of Montmartre with this walking tour. You will be introduced to the likes of Vincent Van Gogh, Pablo Picasso and Henri Toulouse-Lautrec to name a few. The tour includes visits to local art galleries and studios, as well as a visit to the famous Basilica of the Sacred Heart of Paris (Sacre Coeur) and it's stunning viewpoint.

Montmartre Food Tour

Learn about the culinary history of the area with this food tour. Along the way, you will stop at several food stalls and bakeries to taste local delicacies such as croissants, macarons, and artisanal breads while also discovering the local market and the best produce it has to offer.

Montmartre Ghost Tour

Explore the darker side of Montmartre on this spine-tingling walking tour that is said to be haunted by the ghosts of artists and musicians past. You'll visit eerie sites that have been the backdrop for paranormal stories and legends, such as the ghost of the painter Suzanne Valadon who continues to haunt the studio where she created her masterpieces. This tour is not for the faint-hearted!

Montmartre Film Tour

Experience the magic of Montmartre's cinematic history on this walking tour that includes famous movie locations and iconic sites. Follow in the footsteps of Audrey Hepburn and Humphrey Bogart from the classic film "Sabrina" or discover the real-life inspiration for iconic scenes in Oscar-winning movies such as "Amélie" and "Midnight in Paris."

Montmartre Music Tour

Paris has always been a hotbed of musical creativity, and Montmartre was no exception. This walking tour takes you to the famous cabarets and nightclubs where some of the greatest musicians of the 20th century performed — from jazz legends Django Reinhardt and Sidney Bechet to Edith Piaf and Maurice Chevalier. You'll hear about the area's vibrant music scene and learn about the evolution of French music and culture.

Marie

With a passion for art and history, Marie has been a tour guide in Montmartre for over 10 years. She loves introducing visitors to the charming artistic village, sharing stories about famous artists who once called Montmartre home, and offering insider tips to the best local cafes and boutiques.

François

Born and raised in Montmartre, François has a deep knowledge and love for the neighborhood. He specializes in leading visitors off the beaten path to discover hidden gems and local favorites, from quaint alleys and hidden gardens to jazz clubs and traditional wine bars.

Nathalie

Nathalie is a bilingual tour guide who enjoys sharing her French culture and heritage with visitors from around the world. Her Montmartre tours offer a mix of history, art, and local flavor, and she is always happy to answer questions and share personal anecdotes.

Antoine

As an artist and resident of Montmartre, Antoine brings a unique perspective to his tours. He shares fascinating stories about the neighborhood's bohemian past and points out artistic landmarks, offering visitors an authentic and immersive experience of the artistic soul of Montmartre.
